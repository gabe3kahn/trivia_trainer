Post-duel recap — mock

Shown after a head-to-head duel finalizes (both players answered all 6, or it expired). Verdict banner → the two scorelines → clue-by-clue who-got-what (you vs. them) → rematch. Decisions baked in: winner = higher score, then more-correct, then a genuine draw (no time tiebreak); the per-clue timer is the same 30s as the daily challenge; grade self-correction is disabled in duels. Four states below: win, loss, draw, and the in-progress "waiting on them" interstitial.
