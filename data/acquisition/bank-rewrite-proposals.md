## geography (2)

### Argentina `geo-rebuild-001-001`
- **lessons:** Lead with a vivid, concrete hook — not provenance or category labels — Original opens with a bare geographic category label; Andes/Atlantic framing gives the clue a concrete geographic image without changing its difficulty.
- **before:** Occupying most of the Southern Cone, this is South America's second-largest country after Brazil.
- **after:**  Flanked by the Andes to the west and the Atlantic coast to the east, this country occupies most of South America's Southern Cone and is the continent's second-largest after Brazil.

### Bolivia `geo-rebuild-001-002`
- **lessons:** Tighten the wording — 'Spans an Andean west' is grammatically awkward, and 'high-altitude Altiplano' is redundant — Altiplano already means high plateau.
- **before:** This landlocked South American nation spans an Andean west and tropical lowlands, including the high-altitude Altiplano.
- **after:**  This landlocked South American country spans from the Andean Altiplano in the west to tropical lowlands in the east.
